What if it is more open?

Literature

Open access journals and tools to rapidly share the latest discovery.

Research & Analysis

Share your work as you do it with science blogs, open notebooks, and open sourcing your tools.

Review

Post publication, open peer review.

Publish

Web based rapid publishing where the author is in control and owns the copyright.

Citation Management and Sharing

Mendeley: Share your references

  • Automatic citation generation from pdfs
  • Citation database
  • Integration with Word, Open/LibreOffice, BibTeX
  • Track by more than just number of references

Data Sharing/Backup/Version Control

One filesystem to rule them all: Dropbox

  • Synchronizes a folder on your computer to other computers, the web and other users.

Collaborative text editing and coding
